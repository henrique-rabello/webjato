About

Site for everyone!

Webjato was created in 2016 with the objective to democratize the creation of sites and to be channel to support web integration. With the purpose to help entrepeneurs, small business and services. Our main values are:

• to be easy and simple;
• practical and fast;
• compatible with Google;
• large array of applications.

Most of small business, services and common people do not own a site due to the lack of time, money or technical skills to create one. We eliminate these barriers making the technology accessible to everyone, strengthening the market and relation among people.

We are in love, life inspire us and the digital universe bring us together.



Feel free to send us your doubts or sugestions through our support channel.
